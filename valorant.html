Valorant:

Concordia's Valorant team was established in 2022, marking the beginning of their journey. Since then, the team has been making steady progress, with a varsity squad consisting of 5 highly skilled players who have achieved impressive rankings, ranging from Immortal to Gold. The team has participated in the NECC Navigators Divisions for some time now and last semester in spring 2023, they emerged victorious with a remarkable undefeated record of 12-0, making the team a force to be reckoned with. As they gear up for the upcoming season, their goal is to capitalize on their past successes and continue to build on their skills, teamwork, and strategy, with the ultimate aim of becoming an even more formidable team in the future.
