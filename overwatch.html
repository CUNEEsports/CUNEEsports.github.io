Overwatch:

Concordia's Overwatch team was founded in 2020, during the inception of the esports program. Since then, the team has been participating in the NECC Emergents division, and their hard work and dedication have paid off. In the Spring of 2023, the team finally made it to the finals for their division, which was a huge achievement for them. However, the team is not going to rest on their laurels. This year, they are more determined than ever to pursue a division title and bring home the trophy. They are also looking to improve the talent on the roster, as they know that the competition is only going to get tougher. This means that the team is going to be working even harder than before, putting in more hours of training and practice, and pushing themselves to the limit. They are determined to achieve greatness and put Concordia on the map as one of the top esports teams in the country.
