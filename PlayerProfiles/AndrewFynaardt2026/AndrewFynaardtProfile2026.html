Why did you choose Concordia?

I choose Concordia because I wanted a small school with Christian values. I also chose Concordia because of the shooting sports team which is my primary sport. I like the computer science and math programs here as well.

I chose to join esports this year because I enjoy playing games in my free time. The team competed on the games I enjoy playing so I thought I would join the team to try it.

Another hobby I enjoy is shooting sports. Skeet shooting is my favorite but I enjoy sporting as well.

My favorite memory since joining the esports team is meeting new people and playing games with them. I haven’t been on the team for too long but it has been fun so far having teammates to play with.
