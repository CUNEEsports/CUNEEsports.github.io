Personal:

Why did you choose Concordia?

I had multiple friends who went to the school already and my high school promoted it tons. As I looked into it I realized that I liked the school a ton.


Why did you choose Esports?

I’ve been playing video games since I’ve been 10, I started with playing on the Xbox and quickly switched to pc. Esports allowed me to express my love of gaming in a team environment. Being on this esports team has allowed me to meet people and make tons of friends.


What other hobbies do you enjoy?

Some other hobbies I enjoy are going rock climbing and eating foreign food. I’ve been rock climbing since I’ve been playing video games, both hobbies I’ve loved from the first time doing it


Favorite memory about Esports so far?

My favorite moment I’ve had since I have been in esports was when we won the division finals for valorant. We were down in the finals 2-11 on game 5 of valorant, they we started will rounds. We brought it back and won the finals for the division. We ended the season undefeated.
