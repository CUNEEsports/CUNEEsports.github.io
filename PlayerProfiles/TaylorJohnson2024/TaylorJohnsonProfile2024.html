Personal:

Why did you choose Concordia?

I chose Concordia University Nebraska for its small class sizes and personalized academic experience that allows for meaningful relationships with professors. As an IT major, this would be especially beneficial for me to fully engage with the material and excel. Additionally, my sister's positive experience and the university's welcoming atmosphere and strong sense of community confirmed my decision to attend Concordia.


Why did you choose Esports?

I decided to participate in esports because of a particular experience I had in 2020. As soon as the program opened up, some of my friends expressed their interest in checking out the esports room. Intrigued, I decided to join them. Upon entering the room, we found a couple of people playing Rocket League, and I asked if I could join in on the fun. It turned out that the esports team was in need of one more player to complete their Rocket League team, and they asked me to step in as their third player. This experience sparked my interest in esports, and I have been passionately pursuing it ever since.


What other hobbies do you enjoy?

I play golf for Concordia and plan to pursue a career in the golf after college by joining the PGA Apprenticeship after finishing college . In winter, I go snowboarding at a nearby ski resort.


Favorite memory about Esports so far?

One of the most memorable experiences I've had in esports is the people I have met and the friends I have made while playing. Being a part of this community has given me the opportunity to connect with individuals who share my passion for gaming and competition. It's been especially rewarding to be a part of this new and growing community at a small university, where we are able to create and shape the culture around esports. Through all of the late nights practicing, and competing, the relationships I have built with my teammates and fellow gamers are what have made this experience truly special to me.
