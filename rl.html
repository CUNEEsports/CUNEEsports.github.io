Rocket League:

Concordia's Rocket League team was founded in 2020, at the inception of the program. Since then, the team has been making steady progress, with a varsity squad comprising of three players who are ranked around the Champion 1 - 3 skill level. Over the course of their time in the NECC Navigators division, they have achieved several notable victories and have consistently improved their gameplay. This year, the team is looking to take on new challenges as they join a new division, which will undoubtedly test their skills and push them to new heights. Despite the challenges ahead, Concordia's Rocket League team is committed to hard work, dedication, and teamwork, and is excited to see what the future holds for them.
